Everything You Need

Track every aspect of your health in one secure place.

💊

Medications

Track active and past medications, dosages, refill schedules, and check for drug interactions via the FDA database.

🩸

Lab Results

Record lab tests with automatic high/low/normal evaluation. View results in a color-coded matrix across dates.

💙

Conditions

Maintain a history of medical conditions — active, chronic, resolved — with provider notes and family history.

🏥

Care Team

Keep contact details for all your healthcare providers, specialists, and facilities in one organized place.

📋

Reports & PDF Export

Generate comprehensive health reports and travel summaries. Export to PDF or Excel for sharing with providers.

👪

Multi-Patient

Manage health records for your entire family. Separate databases for each person with password protection.

Privacy First. Always.

Your health data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

🔒

On-Device Storage Only

All data stays in a secure SQLite database on your device. No cloud syncing, no remote servers.

🚫

No Accounts Required

No sign-up, no email, no account creation. Start using PHR the moment you download it.

👁

No Tracking or Telemetry

Zero analytics, zero usage tracking. We never see your data and have no way to access it.

📦

You Control Your Backups

Back up to your own storage — external drive, iCloud Drive, wherever you choose. We never touch it.
