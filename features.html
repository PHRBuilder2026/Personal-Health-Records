Features

A comprehensive personal health record — 16 modules to manage every aspect of your health.

Patient Information

Your central health profile with personal details, emergency contacts, blood type, and more. Supports multiple countries with adaptive address and phone formatting.

  • Complete personal health profile
  • Emergency contact information
  • Multi-country address support (US, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, France)
  • Password protection per patient

Medications

Track all your medications — prescriptions, over-the-counter, and supplements. Manage dosages, refill schedules, and check for potential drug interactions.

  • Active and discontinued medication tracking
  • Dosage schedules (daily, weekly, as needed, every-other-day)
  • Refill tracking with pharmacy details
  • FDA drug interaction checker
  • Drug allergy alerts

Lab Results

Record and monitor lab tests over time. PHR automatically evaluates results against normal ranges and highlights values that need attention.

  • Automatic high/low/normal evaluation
  • Color-coded indicators for quick review
  • Panel-based organization (CBC, metabolic, lipid, etc.)
  • Customizable normal ranges
  • Historical trending across dates

Reports & PDF Export

Generate comprehensive health reports combining data from any section. The matrix view shows lab trends at a glance across dates.

  • Combined multi-section health report
  • Lab results matrix view with color indicators
  • Travel health summary for trips
  • Save as PDF with page numbers
  • Export all data to Excel (.xlsx)
